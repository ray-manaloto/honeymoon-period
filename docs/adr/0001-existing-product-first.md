# ADR-0001: Evaluate existing products before custom development

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-07-15

## Context

The desired workflow spans universal iOS capture, collaboration, scoring, structured metadata, time-sensitive priority, and calendar planning. A working Reminders + Beli Shortcut already demonstrates part of the workflow, while several existing products may cover more without the cost and maintenance of a custom app.

## Decision

Run a time-boxed, evidence-based bake-off of existing apps, tools, and composable services before selecting a custom architecture. Preserve the baseline prototype during the bake-off. Compare candidates against one shared rubric and require explicit approval before any paid trial.

## Consequences

- Product research precedes a native-app specification.
- Existing claims must be refreshed from primary sources and tested where material.
- A custom app remains valid only when observed gaps justify its complexity.
- Prototype limitations are evidence, not an automatic mandate to rebuild.

## Revisit when

The bake-off produces an accepted winner, or proves that no candidate meets the required capture, collaboration, scoring, metadata, and planning experience within the approved cost and privacy boundaries.
